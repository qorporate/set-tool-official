Why QSet?

In Nigerian street football, managing sets is a common challenge:

  • Keeping track of multiple teams waiting to play
  • Ensuring fair rotation of teams
  • Managing wins and losses accurately
  • Avoiding disputes over team order

QSet solves these problems by providing a simple, digital solution for managing your football sets.
